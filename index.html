Ancient Magic

Comparatives

To show evolution or compare two things, we forge the adjective with -ER.

Standard

"The responsibility is great."

Forged (Comparative)

"The responsibility is far greater."

Rule

Short Adjective + ER (Strong -> Stronger, Fast -> Faster).

Ancient Magic

Better Than

Some words have chaotic magic. They don't follow the rules. "Good" transforms into BETTER.

Comparing Two People (THAN)

"You must be better than me."

We always use THAN to connect the two things we are comparing.

"Axe is heavier than the sword."

Spartan Law

Modal: MUST

MUST is not an advice (should). It is a strong obligation, a duty, a law.

Duty / Obligation

"We must be better."

Absolute Prohibition

"You must never forget that."
